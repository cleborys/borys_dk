Math Research

My research focused on C*-algebras associated with etale groupoids and the interplay of their Operator-Algebraic and Dynamic properties. Most notably, I developed tools to describe their ideal structure.

Publications

  • Groups, Actions, and C*-Algebras (2020, PhD thesis, ku)
  • The Furstenberg Boundary of a Groupoid (2019, arXiv)
  • A Groupoid Picture of the Elek Algebras (2019, mathematica scandinavica)

Service

  • I was head organizer of the YMC*A 2019 Young Mathematicians in C*-Algebras conference at the University of Copenhagen
  • I gave an outreach talk about Expander Graphs at the Kulturnatten culture night 2018
